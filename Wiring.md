# Pi Pico Wiring
Please see https://pico.pinout.xyz/ for the physical location of pins.
## RFM 96

| RFM 96 | Pi Pico |
| ---| ---|
|VCC | 3V3 Out|
|GND | Ground|
|SCK | SCK (GP 18)|
|MISO | MISO (GP 16)|
|MOSI |	MOSI (GP 19)|
|NSS | GP 10|
|NRESET| GP 9|
|DIO0| GP 2|
